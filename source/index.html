What's P.L.U.G. all about?

Founded by nerds for nerds

P.L.U.G. was created by Linux fans who recognized a lack of student understanding of computer systenms.

Not just a club

P.L.U.G. aims to be a model for future clubs to follow. We strive to inspire students in other school around the country to follow in our footsteps.

Open-source

Everything we do is open-source. Everything from the P.L.U.G. License to this website is open-source and open to peer review.
