What's P.L.U.G. all about?

A helping hand

P.L.U.G. was created by Linux fans who wanted to spread the joy of the kernel.

Not just a club

P.L.U.G. aims to be a model for future clubs to follow. We strive to inspire students in other schools around the country to follow in our footsteps.

Open-source

We plan to contribute a lot to open-source. We've already open-sourced this website, and you're free to take a look at the code.
